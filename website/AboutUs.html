What We Are About

Changing the Face of Recovery provides integrative programs to reinforce sustained recovery for individuals in recovery centers, corporations, and families. Our integrative approach includes restoring physical health and mental/emotional well-being. By empowering the individual to reach a new standard of excellence, they become productive within their relationships, the workforce, and beyond.

It is our goal to bring a fresh and holistic approach to health, wellness, recovery, and employment. Through Functional and Integrative medicine, we will lead our clients through ongoing change with the goal of emotional stability and vibrant health.

Through our community business partners, we will provide unique employment programs for those in recovery. Meaningful employment is key to successful and sustainable recovery. We are a team of passionate professionals with diverse backgrounds and a common goal. We are committed to creating a unique pathway for each individual placing them on course to stable, lifelong recovery.

Changing the Face of Recovery helps individuals struggling with overcoming trauma, renewing passion for life, restoring self respect, family interactions, relationships, reconnecting with the community, giving back, and full recovery.

Our goal is to bring TRUE and lasting recovery to the forefront of current recovery models. To accomplish this, our aim is to provide effective integrative programs to support physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Promoting lasting change and vibrant health through fitness, personalized nutrition, education, specialized coaching, Functional and Integrative medicine, and various emotional healing tools.

When an individual struggles with misuse of alcohol, drugs, food, electronics, emotions, or any number of other unproductive behaviors, an addiction - at its core - is still an addiction. It is just a matter of degree.

Through partnerships with local businesses, our ReEmployment program will be offered to those who have utilized our wellness and recovery programs. The recovering individual is thereby empowered to a new standard of excellence and becomes productive within the workforce and beyond.
