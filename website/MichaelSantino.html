Meet the Team

Michael Santino

After serving in the U S Navy during the Vietnam era, Mike traveled throughout the United States as a computer technician for NCR.

He earned a Bachelors in Business Administration in 1978 and an MBA in 1983. He then became the Manufacturing Manager for several San Diego companies before starting San Marcos Electric.

Currently, Mike works as a DSA Certified Project Inspector on local schools in San Diego County and is volunteering his time as Treasurer for CFR.
