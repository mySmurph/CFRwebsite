Meet the Team

Sudabeh Moein, MD

Dr. Moein is a board certified Ob/Gyn. She graduated from Harbor-UCLA Medical Center with an award as "The Outstanding Resident Surgeon" in 2001. She has been practicing in San Diego, CA since 2007.

After practicing Medicine for several years she felt the need for incorporating Integrative Medicine into her practice in order to optimize her patient's health. She is one of the first few Ob/Gyns in the nation who became board certified by the American Board of Integrative and Holistic Medicine in 2012.

She believes in health education and empowering the patients by providing knowledge to the individual in the community.

She has offered over 50 lectures on women's health and Integrative Medicine to the San Diego community and medical professionals. She has appeared on several local TV stations, has been interviewed by several local newspapers and magazines, and has written several health columns on current topics in her field.

Dr. Moein's integrative approach to patients health has been incorporated into the philosophy of CFR for optimizing their client's recovery.
