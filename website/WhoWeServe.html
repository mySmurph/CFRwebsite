Who We Serve

Individuals and their Families

Recovery comes in all forms and for many reasons. The ultimate goal is the healing of the family unit on all levels. Creating healthy change now and for future generations.

Recovery Centers

Recovery programs put individuals on the path to recovery and a life of sobriety. Our complimentary education teaches healthy nutrition, physical health, and emotional strength. The final goal is employment placement with our community business partners.

Corporations and their Employees

We are not your traditional Corporate Wellness program. Our uniquely designed Corporate Wellness Education program employs world class, professional coaches who have years of experience in their respective health practices. We promote lifestyle management and prevention of disease.

"Recovery occurs via many pathways. Individuals have unique needs, strengths, preferences, goals, culture, backgrounds, and trauma experience that determine their pathways to recovery. Recovery pathways are highly personalized. Recovery is built on the multiple capacities, strengths, talents, coping abilities, resources, and inherent values of each individual.
Recovery is Holistic. Recovery encompasses an individual's whole life, including mind, body, spirit, and community.
Recovery is supported through relationship and social networks. An important factor in the recovery process is the presence and involvement of people who believe in the person's ability to recover. Those who offer hope, support, and encouragement and who also suggest strategies and resources for change. Through these relationships, people leave unhealthy and unfulfilling life roles behind and engage in new roles, e.g., partner, caregiver, friend, student, employee.This leads to a greater sense of belonging, personhood, empowerment, autonomy, social inclusion and community participation." - SAMHSA (transcribed)
