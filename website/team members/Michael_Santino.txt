<h3>Michael Santino</h3>
		<p class="line">
<p>After serving in the U S Navy during the Vietnam era, Mike traveled throughout the United States as a computer technician for NCR. </p>

<p>He earned a Bachelors in Business Administration in 1978 and an MBA in 1983. He then became the Manufacturing Manager for several San Diego companies before starting San Marcos Electric.</p>

<p>Currently, Mike works as a DSA Certified Project Inspector on local schools in San Diego County and is volunteering his time as Treasurer for CFR.</p>
