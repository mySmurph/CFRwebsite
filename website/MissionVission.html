Vision & Mission

Vision

Our vision is to eradicate all forms of addiction relapse. We will design and apply goal-directed health solutions and education for individuals, families, and corporations. Through community involvement and outreach, we will build a bridge for those in recovery to reenter life with passion and purpose.

Mission

Changing the Face of Recovery strives to bring a unique integrative and holistic approach to health, wellness, and full recovery.
