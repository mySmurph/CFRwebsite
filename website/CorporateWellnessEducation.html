Corporate Wellness Education

​Our Corporate Wellness Education division sets us apart from other corporate wellness programs. Our professional team addresses so much more than basic health for your valuable employees. It's not just about cholesterol levels, blood pressure, weight management, and exercise. While those things are important, our primary focus is on revealing, managing and/or rehabilitating employees' lifestyle and medical issues, such as:

  • Gut health and it's connection to healthy brain function
  • Diabetes, cardiac stress, depression, anxiety, obesity
  • The misuse of nicotine, alcohol, marijuana, painkillers, internet, sex, food and gambling
  • Disease management for those who have chronic illnesses such as diabetes, arthritis, chronic pain and hypertension
  • Professionally trained coaches who confidentially unveil potential mental health issues and/or addictive tendencies including: food, sugar, smoking, misuse of alcohol, and prescription/non-prescription drugs. We offer personalized tools for support such as our proprietary mind/body integration techniques and fitness services. This holistic type of support is critical and unique in the Corporate Wellness arena
  • All services are personalized and tailored to meet the specific needs of the individual and the corporation as a whole
